Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for Pro plans?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes! We offer special discounts for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for details.

What is the Fair Selection Algorithm?

Our proprietary algorithm ensures fair and transparent participant selection using cryptographic hashing and priority queues for unbiased results.
